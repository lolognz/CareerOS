# Executive Summary

Senior Backend Software Engineer with several years of experience building Java and Spring Boot microservices, including work in international banking and critical payment systems. He is the kind of engineer who combines backend execution with a long-term view of software quality, placing maintainability, clarity, testing, documentation, and automation at the center of how systems are designed and evolved.

His work is shaped by ownership of complex functional domains and close collaboration with business stakeholders. He is comfortable translating business needs into durable technical designs, asking the necessary questions before implementation, and making decisions that reduce future operational and maintenance cost rather than optimizing only for short-term delivery.

He has a strong interest in software architecture and enjoys designing systems before writing code. His approach favors Clean Code principles, practical engineering discipline, and team collaboration over individual brilliance. Outside his professional work, he continues to create personal technical projects, reinforcing a habit of deliberate practice and independent technical exploration.

For a CTO, his value is not only in backend implementation, but in the judgment he brings to complex systems: thoughtful design, responsible ownership, and a consistent preference for sustainable engineering over shortcuts.
