# CareerOS Philosophy

CareerOS starts from a strong opinion: a resume is the wrong primitive for managing a career.

A resume is useful as an output, but it is a poor source of truth. It is compressed, selective, audience-specific, and usually edited under pressure. Over time, it becomes a fragile document full of omissions, stale phrasing, duplicated facts, and unsupported claims.

## Why Resumes Are Wrong

Resumes force professional experience into a narrow format.

They usually remove:

- Context.
- Constraints.
- Tradeoffs.
- Failed attempts.
- Collaboration.
- Evidence.
- Chronology.
- The reasoning behind decisions.
- The difference between direct ownership and participation.

That compression is sometimes necessary for an application, but it should happen at the end of the process. It should not be the place where the knowledge is stored.

When the resume becomes the source, every future artifact inherits its omissions.

## Why A Professional Knowledge Base Is Better

A Professional Knowledge Base preserves the raw material of a career.

It can hold the full story:

- What happened.
- When it happened.
- Where it happened.
- Who or what it involved.
- What problem existed.
- What actions were taken.
- What technologies and skills were used.
- What changed as a result.
- What evidence supports the claim.
- What the person learned.

This makes the career record more durable than any individual document. It also lets different outputs emphasize different parts of the same truth without rewriting the truth itself.

## Why Outputs Should Be Generated

Every career output has a purpose.

A CV for one role, a LinkedIn summary, a portfolio project page, a conference bio, and an interview answer may all draw from the same experience, but they should not be manually maintained as separate sources.

Manual editing creates drift. Generated outputs reduce drift by making the transformation explicit:

```text
source knowledge -> selection -> formatting -> output
```

If the output is wrong, the fix belongs upstream. Either the source knowledge is incomplete, the selection logic is wrong, or the generation prompt is weak. Editing the generated artifact directly only hides the problem until the next output is needed.

## The Role Of AI

AI is useful when it is grounded.

In CareerOS, AI should help organize, connect, summarize, transform, and generate from known facts. It should not invent the facts themselves.

The knowledge base gives AI agents a boundary: every generated statement must be supported by source material. When the source is insufficient, the correct behavior is to ask for more knowledge or produce a narrower output.

## The Core Principle

The source is the career system.

Everything else is a view.
