# CareerOS Constitution

## Mission

CareerOS exists to become the single source of truth for an entire professional career.

Its purpose is to preserve professional knowledge in a structured, durable, and reusable form: experience, projects, companies, roles, skills, technologies, publications, evidence, chronology, decisions, stories, and the relationships between them.

CareerOS is not a resume repository. Resumes, CVs, bios, websites, portfolios, interview answers, cover letters, and profile summaries are generated views over the knowledge base. They are outputs, not sources of truth.

## Core Principles

- Source over output.
- Facts over narrative.
- Evidence over claims.
- Relationships over duplication.
- Evolution over perfection.
- Human-first, AI-accelerated.
- Every generated artifact is disposable.
- Preserve chronology.
- Never invent.
- Prefer explicit links over copied text.

The knowledge base must contain the facts needed to support any generated artifact. If a fact is missing, the correct response is to identify the gap, not to fill it with plausible language.

## AI Rules

AI agents must read before writing.

They must update canonical knowledge before creating or changing outputs. Generated artifacts must never be edited as if they were the source of truth.

AI agents must preserve traceability between facts, evidence, entities, chronology, and generated views. They should prefer structured knowledge over polished prose when capturing source material.

When information is incomplete, ambiguous, or unsupported, AI agents must ask for clarification instead of guessing.

## Human Rules

Human contributors should treat CareerOS as a living knowledge system.

They should add raw facts, evidence, dates, context, examples, decisions, and relationships as close to the source as possible. They should avoid turning source knowledge into premature marketing language or resume-style summaries unless explicitly generating an output.

Humans should correct the canonical knowledge when something changes, then regenerate or revise outputs from that source. They should preserve their own voice, context, and uncertainty where those details may matter later.

## Versioning Philosophy

Careers evolve. CareerOS must reflect that evolution without rewriting history to make it look cleaner than it was.

Past roles, decisions, descriptions, evidence, and interpretations should remain traceable. Corrections and refinements are welcome, but they should clarify the record rather than erase meaningful history.

The system should support growth, change, and reinterpretation while preserving chronology and evidence.

## Long-Term Vision

CareerOS is a lifelong professional operating system.

It should help a person understand, maintain, and reuse the full body of their professional knowledge across years, roles, industries, projects, and goals.

Its highest value is not producing a single polished document. Its value is making a career legible, trustworthy, connected, and reusable for any future context.
