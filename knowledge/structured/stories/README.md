# Stories

This folder contains structured story entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Concrete professional examples with context, situation, action, result, and evidence.
- Interview-ready narratives grounded in raw knowledge.
- References to companies, roles, projects, skills, technologies, principles, and raw interview fragments.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Generic summaries of a whole career.
- Claims invented to make a story stronger.
- Full project documentation.
- Generated interview answers unless explicitly stored as disposable output elsewhere.

## References

Story entities should link to the entities they demonstrate instead of repeating all surrounding context.

Every story should reference at least one raw fragment or evidence source.

## Why This Exists

Stories preserve specific examples that demonstrate judgment, behavior, tradeoffs, learning, and impact.
