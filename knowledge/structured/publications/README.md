# Publications

This folder contains structured publication entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Articles, papers, books, reports, essays, newsletters, public documents, and other authored or co-authored works.
- Publication metadata, topic, venue, role, date, evidence, and related professional context.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Draft marketing copy for generated outputs.
- Talks unless they were also published as a distinct artifact.
- Claims about impact that are not supported by raw knowledge or evidence.

## References

Publication entities should reference related projects, companies, roles, stories, skills, technologies, talks, awards, and raw interview fragments.

## Why This Exists

Publications preserve externally visible professional knowledge and provide evidence for expertise, communication, and domain understanding.
