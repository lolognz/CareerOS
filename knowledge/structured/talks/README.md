# Talks

This folder contains structured talk entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Presentations, lectures, workshops, interviews, panels, podcasts, and public speaking events.
- Event metadata, topic, audience, role, date, evidence, and related professional context.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Publications unless they are separate artifacts.
- Generated speaker bios.
- Unsupported audience size, reach, or impact claims.
- Raw transcripts that belong in raw knowledge.

## References

Talk entities should reference related projects, companies, roles, stories, skills, technologies, publications, awards, and raw interview fragments.

## Why This Exists

Talks preserve public communication evidence and connect expertise to audiences, topics, and professional context.
