# Companies

This folder contains structured company entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Organizations where professional work, study, publication, speaking, or collaboration happened.
- Stable company metadata such as name, type, industry, location, website, and time range.
- Context needed to understand work performed within or for the organization.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Detailed project narratives. Put those in `projects/`.
- Role-specific responsibilities. Put those in `roles/`.
- Story-level examples. Put those in `stories/`.
- Generated resume or profile wording.
- Facts not traceable to raw knowledge.

## References

Company entities should reference related roles, projects, stories, skills, technologies, publications, talks, awards, and raw interview fragments by stable IDs.

Prefer references over copying details from those entities.

## Why This Exists

Companies provide organizational context. They help generators understand where work happened without making each project or story repeat the same background.
