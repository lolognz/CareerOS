# Roles

This folder contains structured role entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Formal or functional roles held during a time period.
- Responsibilities, scope, reporting context, seniority, team context, and time range.
- References to companies, projects, stories, skills, technologies, awards, and raw interview fragments.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Company-wide context unrelated to the role.
- Detailed project implementation notes.
- Generic skill descriptions.
- Generated job titles created for positioning.
- Facts not supported by raw knowledge.

## References

Role entities should reference the company where the role occurred and any projects, stories, skills, technologies, or outcomes connected to that role.

## Why This Exists

Roles describe professional responsibility and scope over time. They keep chronology clear and prevent projects from carrying all employment context.
