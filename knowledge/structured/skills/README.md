# Skills

This folder contains structured skill entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Professional capabilities demonstrated by evidence.
- Definitions, scope, proficiency context, and examples of use.
- References to projects, roles, stories, technologies, education, publications, talks, awards, and raw interview fragments.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Unsupported claims of expertise.
- Technology entries that should be in `technologies/`.
- Resume keyword stuffing.
- Generated phrasing detached from evidence.

## References

Skill entities should reference the work that demonstrates them. Prefer evidence-bearing links to projects, stories, and roles.

## Why This Exists

Skills make capabilities reusable across outputs while keeping them grounded in demonstrated work.
