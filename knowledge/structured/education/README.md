# Education

This folder contains structured education entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Degrees, programs, courses, certifications, workshops, training, self-study tracks, and academic experiences.
- Dates, institutions, topics, projects, evidence, and related skills or technologies.
- References to companies, projects, stories, publications, talks, awards, skills, technologies, and raw interview fragments.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Unsupported credentials.
- Full transcripts unless intentionally stored as evidence.
- Generated education summaries for resumes.
- Skills not supported by education or other raw knowledge.

## References

Education entities should reference related skills, technologies, projects, publications, awards, and raw source fragments.

## Why This Exists

Education captures formal and informal learning that supports professional knowledge and explains how capabilities were developed.
