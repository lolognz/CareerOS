# Projects

This folder contains structured project entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Professional, academic, open-source, research, entrepreneurial, or internal initiatives.
- Project context, goals, constraints, role, architecture, decisions, outcomes, and evidence.
- References to companies, roles, stories, skills, technologies, publications, talks, awards, and raw interview fragments.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Full company history. Put that in `companies/`.
- Generic skill definitions. Put those in `skills/`.
- Long raw narratives. Keep those in raw knowledge.
- Generated portfolio or resume copy.
- Unsupported claims.

## References

Project entities should link to company, role, story, skill, technology, publication, talk, award, and raw source IDs.

Do not duplicate long descriptions from referenced entities.

## Why This Exists

Projects are the main unit of demonstrated professional work. They connect problems, actions, tools, decisions, outcomes, and evidence.
