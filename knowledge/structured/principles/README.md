# Principles

This folder contains structured professional principle entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Durable beliefs, operating principles, decision criteria, values, and professional philosophy.
- Evidence showing where the principle was practiced or learned.
- References to stories, projects, roles, companies, skills, technologies, and raw interview fragments.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Generic values copied from common career advice.
- Aspirational claims with no evidence.
- Generated personal-brand language.
- Principles that contradict raw knowledge without explanation.

## References

Principle entities should reference concrete stories and projects that demonstrate the principle in action.

## Why This Exists

Principles explain how a person thinks and works. They help generated outputs preserve voice, judgment, and consistency without inventing a persona.
