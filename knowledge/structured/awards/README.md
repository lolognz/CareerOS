# Awards

This folder contains structured award and recognition entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Awards, honors, recognitions, scholarships, grants, rankings, nominations, and notable acknowledgements.
- Issuer, date, criteria, context, evidence, and related work.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Informal praise without enough context unless stored as evidence elsewhere.
- Unsupported prestige claims.
- Generated achievement bullets.
- Metrics or claims not present in raw knowledge.

## References

Award entities should reference related companies, roles, projects, stories, skills, technologies, education, publications, talks, and raw interview fragments.

## Why This Exists

Awards provide evidence of external recognition and should remain connected to the work or context that earned them.
