# Roles

This folder contains role entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Formal or functional roles held during a specific period.
- Scope, responsibilities, team context, seniority, and relationship to a company or organization.
- Links to projects, stories, skills, technologies, and achievements demonstrated during the role.

## Naming Convention

Use a stable lowercase slug that combines role and context:

```text
role-title-company-or-context.md
```

When dates are needed to disambiguate, include a year:

```text
role-title-company-YYYY.md
```

## Relationship With Other Entities

Roles connect chronology to work. A role may reference one company and many projects or stories. Projects and stories should reference the role when they happened within that responsibility period.
