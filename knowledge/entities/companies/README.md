# Companies

This folder contains company entities.

## What Belongs Here

- Organizations where work, study, collaboration, publishing, speaking, or recognition happened.
- Organization-level context such as business, industry, products, market, location, and website.
- Links to related roles, projects, stories, skills, technologies, publications, talks, and achievements.

## Naming Convention

Use a stable lowercase slug:

```text
company-name.md
```

If the organization has a common acronym or short name, use the clearest canonical name and record alternatives in `aliases`.

## Relationship With Other Entities

Companies provide context. They should link to roles and projects instead of duplicating role responsibilities or project details. Stories, talks, publications, and achievements may reference companies when the relationship is supported by source knowledge.
